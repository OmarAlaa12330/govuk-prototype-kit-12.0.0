Tools

As a business analyst, you are required to provide data and flowcharts to others in order to assist with the stakeholder requirements.
We will be going over the main tools used and the methods applied in order to deliver a high-quality documentation.

Communication is key

Communication platforms and software can be indispensable in faciitating better collaboration and connection in-house as well as with stakeholders and colleagues.
Examples of these platforms : Slack, Teams, Google Workspace, and Zoom.

Presenting

Presentations enable business analyst to show data, in a clear, succinct, an targeted way. Stakeholders, while keen to understand why decisions are made, are largely unfamiliar with the complexity and nunces of the data itself.
Examples of these platforms: Microsoft PowerPoint, Google Slides, Canva and Prezi.
