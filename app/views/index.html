Who are we? / What is a Business Analyst?

Business Analysts understand and analyse user and business needs to provide the team with the required information inorder to deliver the solution needed.

What we do?

As Business Analyst, our main rule is to:

  • work with stakeholders to investigate servce, function or product provided

  • produce written documentation, report our findings

  • support the staff and team in making recommended changes, including helping to resolve any issues

  • Ensure plans are made and processes are created to evaluate the impact of the changes made

  • gain agreement, usually from senior management, of the best method of introducing the recommendations to the stakeholders
