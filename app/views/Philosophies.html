Philosofies

This page will show the philosofies of the Business Analyst in the Civil Service. As they play an important role in merging into the team and simplifying the cooperation between team members. Another importance is that following a philosofy in the workplace ensures that you are following the policy and regulations put in place.

Question everything

A business analyst must question everything he encounters to break down any complex topics into smaller achievable bits. This also simplifies delivering concepts to the rest of the teams and the stakeholders.

Uncover any gaps remaining

It is important that if there are any gaps appearing through the prcess, the buiness analyst seeks to uncover them and try to fill them with the required information.

Effective translation

As a business analyst works with various people with various levels of technicality, it is important that he breaks down complex words or terms into easier information for non-technical people.

User-centered design process

It is important that all the steps of the project have been builf and/or created based aroun user necessity.
